Worldwide distribution of the 5,565 islands with native monocot species and their classification according to four categories of vulnerability (low, moderate, high, very high) to threats related to climate change: ( a) sea level rise ( b) shift in temperature, ( c) shift in rainfall, and ( d) global vulnerability to climate change.
