Simulated upper- and lower-level circulation anomalies in response to each forcing. Upper (σ = 0.230) and lower troposphere (σ = 0.950) streamfunction anomalies (contour; m 2 s −1) averaged over 25–29 days for the ( a,b) SASM, ( c,d) EASM, and ( e,f) Atlantic experiments. The intervals of streamfunction anomalies are in ( a,c) 1.2 × 10 6 m 2 s −1, ( b,d) 0.2 × 10 6 m 2 s −1, ( e) 0.5 × 10 6 m 2 s −1, and ( f) 0.15 × 10 6 m 2 s −1. Orange (sky blue) shading in ( a,c) indicates the upper-level zonal wind anomaly greater (less) than 0.5 (–0.5) m s −1. Blue lines denote the Rossby wave ray path for waves with the zonal wavenumber 5. Maps were generated using GrADS version 1.9b4 (http://grads.iges.org/grads/).
