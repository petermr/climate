Remotely sensed reflectance. Current day composite (1998–2015) for a model R RS interpolated to 443 nm, b observed R RS at 443 nm, c model R RS interpolated to 555 nm, and d observed R RS at 555 nm. Units are sr −1. Observed fields are from the Ocean Colour Climate Change Initiative (OC-CCI) project. White areas are regions where model resolution is too coarse to capture the smaller seas or regions of constant ice cover.
