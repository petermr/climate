Distribution and images of the studied species. Limits of the Chihuahuan Desert and distribution map and images of the five plant species studied. Main biogeographic areas are indicated in addition to occurrence localities. Areas follow Shreve (1942) and Morafka (1977). (A) Chihuahuan Desert subprovinces and distribution of studied species. (B) Delimitation of the Chihuahuan Desert. (C) Berberis trifoliolata (Berberidaceae). (D) Ephedra aspera (Ephedraceae). (E) Setchellanthus caeruleus (Setchellanthaceae). (F) Leucophyllum laevigatum (Scrophulariaceae). (G) Lindleya mespiloides (Rosaceae). Images taken by Diego F. Angulo, Israel Loera, Victoria Sosa, Etelvina Gándara and Marilyn Vásquez-Cruz, respectively.
