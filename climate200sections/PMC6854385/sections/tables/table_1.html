Note

The estimates are the mean ±  SD and 95% credible intervals of the posterior distribution of the difference of 2017 relative to 1993. Thus negative estimates show that arrival and egg‐laying dates were earlier in 2017, and the raw arrival‐breeding interval was longer than in 1993. The p (2017 < 1993) shows the probability that values in 2017 were earlier (or shorter) than in 1993.
