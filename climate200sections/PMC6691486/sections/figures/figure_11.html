Changes in optimal performance areas within 1 h on 23 June 2017. Blue–brown represents optimal performance areas at 14:00 EDT, while blue represents optimal performance areas at 15:00 EDT. Orange represents areas of sub-lethal or lethal performance. Relative performance is based on an intertidal organism TPC with a T opt of 28°C. Temporal comparisons of relative performance can be an opportunity to describe thermal behaviour for mobile species.
