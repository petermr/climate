Figure 3

Map of sea ice extent when Se = 1 ± 0.05 (the brown line shows the NSIDC ice edge and the turquoise patch is from the CMIP5 models). The yellow patch shows the CMIP5 distribution of sea ice extent when the total extent decreases to (1 ± 0.6) × 10 6 km 2. In each case, the composite multi-model mean distribution is shown. The map was created using MATLAB R2016b software ( www.mathworks.com/products/matlab.html) and the M_Map mapping package ( www.eoas.ubc.ca/~rich/map.html).
